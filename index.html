Health Tips

schedule
Take Medicines on Time

Set a daily alarm for every medicine you take. Skipping or delaying doses — especially for BP, diabetes or thyroid — can seriously reduce their effectiveness. Consistency is the key to treatment success.

water_drop
Stay Hydrated

Drink at least 8–10 glasses of water every day. Proper hydration helps your kidneys flush out toxins, supports better medicine absorption, and prevents common issues like headaches, fatigue and kidney stones.

directions_run
Move Every Day

Even 30 minutes of brisk walking can lower blood pressure, regulate blood sugar, and strengthen your heart. Regular physical activity reduces your dependence on medicines over time and boosts your mental health too.

no_meals
Avoid Self-Medication

Never purchase antibiotics, sleeping pills or strong painkillers on your own. Wrong medicines can mask serious conditions, cause harmful side effects, or lead to antibiotic resistance. Always consult a pharmacist or doctor first.

bedtime
Prioritise Sleep

Your body heals and repairs itself during sleep. Getting 7–8 hours each night strengthens your immune system, improves memory, regulates hormones, and speeds up recovery from illness. Poor sleep can worsen chronic conditions.

thermostat
Store Medicines Correctly

Always keep medicines in a cool, dry place away from direct sunlight. Never store them in the bathroom or near the kitchen stove — heat and humidity degrade medicines. Check expiry dates monthly and dispose of expired ones safely.

restaurant
Eat a Balanced Diet

Include fresh fruits, vegetables, whole grains and proteins in every meal. A nutritious diet reduces inflammation, controls cholesterol and sugar levels, and can lower your need for long-term medicines. Food is your first medicine.

monitor_heart
Monitor Your Vitals Regularly

If you have diabetes, hypertension or a heart condition, check your BP and blood sugar at home regularly. Early detection of changes allows your doctor to adjust treatment before a problem becomes serious. Prevention is better than cure.

Yes! We offer free home delivery within 2 KM from 11 AM to 11 PM. For areas beyond 2 KM, minimal delivery charges apply. We also provide nationwide courier services.

Absolutely. We are a licensed pharmacy and stock only genuine medicines from authorized distributors. All products come with proper packaging and batch details.
